PURCHASE & DONATE

Why we need your donations?

By visiting our sanctuary and generously donating, you are directly contributing to the betterment of turtles and their habitats. Your valuable contributions enable us to implement vital conservation initiatives, including nesting beach protection, research projects, and community outreach programs. With your support, we can enhance nesting site management, conduct educational campaigns, promote sustainable fishing practices, and develop crucial turtle conservation infrastructure. Together, we are making a lasting impact on the survival and well-being of these incredible creatures, ensuring a brighter future for turtles and the ecosystems they inhabit. Your donation truly makes a difference in our mission to protect and conserve turtles for generations to come.
